About Artworks

Art is the story of humanity — from cave drawings to modern installations. Our gallery showcases artworks from various cultures and time periods:

  • 🖼 Renaissance Art – Michelangelo, Leonardo da Vinci
  • 📜 Medieval Art – Gothic & Byzantine styles
  • 🎨 Modern Art – Abstract, Cubism, Expressionism
  • 💡 Contemporary Art – Digital & Installation art
